Leh - ladakh

Ladakh is a paradise for adventure enthusiasts and culture lovers alike. Key attractions include the Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Tso Moriri Lake, and the monasteries such as Hemis, Thiksey, and Lamayuru. Motorbiking and cycling over the high mountain passes, trekking in the rugged terrain, and river rafting in the Indus are popular activities.

Ladakh is nestled between the Kunlun mountain range in the north and the main Great Himalayas to the south. It is a high-altitude desert, characterized by its stark beauty, arid mountains, and crystal-clear waters.
